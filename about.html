About Us

Founded in 2022, FORESTRY WATCH ZAMBIA is a none Govermental Organization working to suppliment efforts of the Zambian Goverment to fight climatic change through provision of Enviromental Education for Communities neighbring with the Natural Resource. Our Forestry programs contributes to conservation and rehabilitation of natural resources to establish, manage and sustainably use forests. Our programs provide scientific support to understand the role of forestry and its potential to improve livelihoods of smallholder farmers and their communities. It focuses on aspects of forestry value chains that have good economic potential, and where the benefits will have early benefits for communities. Specifically, the forestry program aims to:

  • Improve livelihoods of smaller holders and communities by enhancing sustainable land and forest management
  • Foster economic growth through legal, sustainable and value-adding forest based industries .
  • Contribute to the Zambian Government's forest-policy priorities in combating illegal logging and reducing deforestation.

Areas of focus include development of silvicultural systems, forest restoration, improved germplasm, harvesting and processing approaches, and the management of threats.

Moringa Tree - benefits
Moringa oleifera is a plant native to northern India that can also grow in other tropical and sub-tropical places, like Asia and Africa. Folk medicine has used the leaves, flowers, seeds, and roots of this plant for centuries. It's traditionally been used as a remedy for such conditions as:
  • Diabetes
  • Long-lasting inflammation
  • Bacterial, viral, and fungal infections
  • pain
  • Heart health
  • Cancer
