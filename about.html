Work Experience

By July of 2010, the Author found his first job as a Center agent in the vibrant telecommunications industry with a company call Zain Zambia. It was not long before the company changes its name to AIRTEL ZAMBIA. Since then, the author was priveledged to have held a number of positions within the call center function which included among others;

  • Call Center agent - Main Call Center
  • Data Help Desk specialist
  • Team leader - Main Call Center
  • Team leader - Back office

In February 2019, the author left AIRTEL ZAMBIA to join an upcoming Solar company called Kazang Solar in the customer service department as a call center agent. A year after which he was promoted to the position of call center supervisor under the credit control function, a position held for close t 2 years. Later in March of 2022 he was again promoted to the position of Customer Service Manager, a position he still holds todate.
